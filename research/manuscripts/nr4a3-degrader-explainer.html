How we're hunting for the drug — step by step

The cancer is driven by one broken protein. The goal: a molecule that throws it in the bin. Here's the actual pipeline — all on a computer.

STEP 1
Build the model

Predict its shape

No real 3D structure exists, so AI builds one.

STEP 2
Check the pocket

Is there a way in?

Measure it against known drugs — it looks shut.

STEP 3

STEP 4

Spot what's unique

Compare to its twins for handholds only it has.

STEP 5
Test-fit molecules

Try the keys

Dock a library + AI-design new ones; rank them.

NEXT

Make & test it
