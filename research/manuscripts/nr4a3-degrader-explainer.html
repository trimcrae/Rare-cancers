Designing a medicine for a rare cancer — on a computer

The cancer is driven by one broken protein. The plan: throw that protein in the bin.

The problem

Stuck "on"

One protein is jammed on, driving the tumour.

Why it's hard

No keyhole

Normal drugs need a pocket. This one is shut.

The idea

Bin it

A "degrader" hands it to the cell's trash.

The find

Door flickers open

Simulations: it cracks open ~1 in 4 moments.

Make it safe

Hit only the bad one

Spare its near-identical twins.
