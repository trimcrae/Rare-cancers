# Medical disclaimer

The Rare Cancer Info Hub is an educational, patient-built project. It is **not
medical advice** and is **not a substitute for professional medical care**.

- Information here is gathered from publicly available literature and may be
  incomplete, out of date, or not applicable to your situation.
- Nothing on this site should be used to make diagnosis, treatment, or
  monitoring decisions. Always consult a qualified clinician — ideally a
  specialist sarcoma/oncology centre — about your own care.
- The "what happened to people like me?" tool summarises small, pooled datasets
  and, where marked, **illustrative sample data**. Its numbers are a rough
  signal only and are **not a prediction** for any individual.
- Treatment and monitoring frameworks reflect general published principles at
  the time of review; drugs, sequencing, and availability vary by country and
  change over time.
- Links to external sites, support groups, and centres are provided for
  convenience and do not imply endorsement.

If you think you may have a medical emergency, contact your local emergency
services immediately.
